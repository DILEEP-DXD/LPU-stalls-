Our Specials

Paratha

Flaky, layered Indian bread, often stuffed with spiced potatoes or paneer.

Golgappa (Pani Puri)

Crispy puris filled with spicy, tangy water and potato filling.

Poha

Flattened rice cooked with turmeric, mustard seeds, and topped with fresh coriander and sev.

Chole Bhature

Spicy chickpeas served with deep-fried, fluffy bread.

Samosa

Spiced potato filling wrapped in a crispy pastry shell, deep-fried to perfection.

Kachori

Flaky, deep-fried pastry filled with spiced lentils or peas.

Aloo Tikki

Crispy fried potato patties, often served with tamarind and mint chutney.

Dahi Bhalla

Soft lentil dumplings served in spiced yogurt, topped with chutney.

Pav Bhaji

Spicy mashed vegetables served with buttered bread rolls.

Jalebi

Deep-fried flour spirals soaked in saffron-flavored sugar syrup.
